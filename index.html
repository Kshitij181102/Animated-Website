WHO IS A PENETRATION TESTER

A penetration tester is a cybersecurity professional who assesses computer systems, networks, and applications for vulnerabilities. They simulate attacks to identify weaknesses, providing insights to enhance security measures. Utilizing ethical hacking techniques, they aim to preemptively detect and mitigate potential risks, safeguarding against malicious exploitation and data breaches.
